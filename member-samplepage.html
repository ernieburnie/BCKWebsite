YUKI CHIU '18 Marketing Director

Yuki is a sophomore at Dominican International School and an executive officer of the school’s Model United Nations club. Through attending MUN conferences, Yuki loves to express her own feelings and opinions to others; furthermore, she is eager to build up relationships with as many committees as she can. Being a student council member, Yuki seeks to bring her communication and organizing skills to the TEDxDominicanIntlSchool team; having said that, she wishes to the members in the team to gather each other's experience (also idea) and host an unforgettable event together.



What is your favorite TED talk and why?


This, “Your body Language Shapes Who You Are”, is my favorite Ted Talk. In this talk, Amy Cuddy has been well-organized the whole speech, from the evidence of her experiences to her own story. She has showed the fact that the posture can change the way of thinking. Even a less confident person, he or she can use the knowledge of doing high power poses to increase his or her amount of testosterone; all we need is 2 minutes, our bodies, and a private place. Just as Amy Cuddy has said, “fake it, till you become it”. To put it briefly, body language can surely change us to the person that we want to be.
