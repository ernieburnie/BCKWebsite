Attendee Policy

Any matters not specifically covered by these Attendee Policies shall be subject to the decision of TEDxDominicanIntlSchool. The team may amend or add further policies. All the changes made shall be binding on all attendees along with the Attendee Policies.


Conduct

By attending, you agree to comply with all the regulations set forth by the TEDxDominicanIntlSchool team. TEDxDominicanIntlSchool reserves the right to refuse admittance to, or remove any person from the event at any time, including but not limited to attendees behaving in an inappropriate, disorderly, disrespectful, or dangerous manner or failing to comply with the aforementioned terms and condition. Please report any concerns, offensive behavior, or suspicious or disruptive activity to the nearest uniformed staff member.


Location

The TEDxDominicanIntlSchool event will take place at Dominican International School in Taipei, Taiwan. We do not provide shuttle buses or any other transportation services. Attendees are responsible for their own transportation to Dominican International School. The TEDxDominicanIntlSchool reserves the right to change venues due to weather, capacity, or any other issues without notice and without liability.


Badges

During registration, attendees will be issued an attendee badge that will grant them access to venue areas. Attendees must wear their badge at all times. If requested by any TEDxDominicanIntlSchool staff member, attendees may be required to provide additional identification.
Badges are not replaceable or transferable. Unless otherwise agreed by TEDxDominicanIntlSchool, the full ticket fee will be charged to replace a lost badge. Attendees cannot share, transfer, give, or provide their badge to individuals other than themselves.
We are not responsible for any badges or property that is lost, stolen, damaged or destroyed at the event. Your badge may be invalidated if we suspect that you have altered your badge in any way.


Venue Area Regulations

Please keep your volume at a minimum inside the venue area to prevent causing interruptions. Keep your cellphone off or set it to silent mode during the talks so that it does not disrupt the speaker. Food and drinks are strictly prohibited inside venue areas, and littering will not be tolerated on all campus grounds. Pictures are allowed outside of the venue area. When taking photos, attendees should be mindful of other guests.


Film and Video Consent

Photographs, audio and/or video taken at the event by TEDxDominicanIntlSchool, or others on behalf of our event, may include your voice, image or likeness. You agree that TEDxDominicanIntlSchool may use such photographs, audio and/or video, including those that may contain your voice, image or likeness, for any purpose on a worldwide basis in perpetuity, without any compensation to you, and you release TEDxDominicanIntlSchool from all liability related thereto.


No Photography, Smoking, or Soliciting

You are not permitted to make audio or audiovisual recordings of the talks, take professional photographic or video equipment, or wearable recording devices into venue areas. In addition, you may not use selfie sticks or similar monopods on campus grounds. Solicitation or selling of items or services is not allowed at the event. Any attendee conducting these activities may be removed. Smoking (including e-cigarettes) is not permitted on campus grounds.


In Case of an Emergency

For emergency services, dial 119 (fire) or 110 (police) from telecommunication devices or find a uniformed staff member.


Fire

In case of a fire, attendees must leave the building immediately by walking to the nearest safe exit. Leave all belongings. Do not use the elevators. Do not return to the building until authorities indicate that it is safe to do so.


Earthquakes

Should an earthquake occur, remain calm and take cover under a stable object. Face away from glass, unstable objects, and tall structures. Remain where you are until the shaking stops and wait for further instructions from authorities. If directed to evacuate the venue area, please walk to the nearest exit. Do not use the elevators and do not return to the building until authorities indicate that it is safe to do so.


Security

Every precaution is taken to ensure that the TEDxDominicanIntlSchool event is a safe and secure learning environment for attendees. Event staff members are present at all times on campus grounds. TEDxDominicanIntlSchool is not responsible for any missing items. Any items left unattended around the event space will likely be picked up by a TEDxDominicanIntlSchool staffer and be taken to the Lost and Found area.


Additional Tips

Please arrive on time, prepare to get your minds blown, and support our speakers by giving your warmest applauses to the speakers after each talk!
