VOLUNTEER











"For students, by students."

Do you want to play a part in the 2016 TEDxDominicanIntlSchool? Are you thinking of how to get involved?
Volunteering is a great way for passionate students to contribute to our actions. The team is open to any and all efforts by enthusiastic students all around campus; we hope to provide a medium for a sharing of new and innovative ideas in various fields. As a youth event, we would like to address different areas of potential interest. After all, the team, like any willing individual, consists of a group of students, working for a belief in the widespread sharing of new ideas. Our vision is to provide our volunteers a canvas to showcase their skills to the community and for the community.
